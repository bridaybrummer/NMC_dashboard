TipHow to generate reports

Last 4 weeks (default):

source("weekly-reports/run_all_weekly.R")

Adjust number of weeks — edit N_WEEKS at the top of run_all_weekly.R before running.

Then run quarto render or source("dev/deploy.r") to publish. A listing stub is created automatically in weekly-reports/posts/ for each rendered week.
